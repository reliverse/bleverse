Welcome to **Awesome Bleverse**.

This is not just a list of repositories. It is a record of people who build with intention. Because Bleverse is more than a framework. It is an evolving architectural calm, modular, and creator-first philosophy. And every project listed here expands that philosophy in its own way.

Every listed project carries a piece of its [creator's soul](/blog/soul). If some project resonates with you, please support it: star the repository, contribute, or sponsor the maintainers.

## The Builders

These projects use Bleverse directly: in production, in experiments, in real systems touched by real users.

- [Bleverse](https://github.com/reliverse/bleverse): The official platform and a living example of the framework in action.

> Want your project featured here? Open a PR and tell us what you're building.

## The Shoulders We Stand On

No meaningful system is built in isolation.

Bleverse learns from the open-source world. From projects that value clarity over chaos, architecture over hacks, and thoughtfulness over noise.

We study them. We borrow patterns. We adapt ideas. We say huge thank you to them. With respect.

These projects shaped parts of Bleverse's thinking.

- [mugnavo/tanstarter](https://github.com/mugnavo/tanstarter): A minimal TanStack Start template powered by Better Auth, Drizzle ORM, and shadcn/ui.
- [vercel-labs/knowledge-agent-template](https://github.com/vercel-labs/knowledge-agent-template): An open-source file-system and knowledge-based agent template.

## The Ecosystem

Bleverse is part of something larger.

Tools, templates, communities. They are all pushing toward better developer experience and more intentional systems.

- [@tanstack/create-start](https://github.com/TanStack/create-tsrouter-app/blob/main/cli/ts-create-start/README.md): The TanStack's CLI tool for creating Start projects.
- [awesome-tanstack-start](https://github.com/Balastrong/awesome-tanstack-start): A curated collection of resources around TanStack Start.
