### ▸ TYPE

Biological Entity — Modified / Anomaly-Induced

### ▸ CHANCE TO ENCOUNTER

Relic

### ▸ DEFINITION

An organism that has undergone significant mutation due to Bleverse exposure, genetic intervention, or ontological resonance.

### ▸ CHARACTERISTICS

- Unstable biology
- Unpredictable behavioral patterns
- Hybrid or recursive anatomy
- Potential for rapid adaptation or replication

### ▸ SUBTYPES

- Failed Subjects
- Bio-sync Hosts
- Cognitive Mimics

### ▸ RISK LEVEL

**Class III – Bio-Ontological Hazard**
Containment requires constant re-evaluation. Use multi-spectrum inhibitors.

### ▸ NOTES

Mutation is not always external. Internal rewiring has been documented prior to visible change.
