### ▸ TYPE

Biological Entity — Non-Sapient

### ▸ CHANCE TO ENCOUNTER

Incredibly easy

### ▸ DEFINITION

An entity of organic origin lacking independent sapience. Includes both natural and anomalously affected lifeforms.

### ▸ CHARACTERISTICS

- Instinct-driven behavior
- Limited or no resistance to cognitive anomalies
- May become hosts to biological or ontological contamination

### ▸ SUBTYPES

- Earth-native fauna
- Anomaly-induced chimeras
- Symbiotic derivatives

### ▸ RISK LEVEL

**Class I – Low Threat**
Containment is generally biological in nature.

### ▸ NOTES

Animals may serve as vectors for anomalous transmission. Monitoring recommended in exposed zones.
