### ▸ TYPE

Object-Class Anomalous Construct

### ▸ CHANCE TO ENCOUNTER

Relic

### ▸ DEFINITION

An Artifact is a non-living object that exhibits anomalous properties, influence, or effects.
It may be dormant, reactive, sentient, or recursive in function. Some artifacts carry embedded consciousness or memory layers.

### ▸ CHARACTERISTICS

- Not inherently alive, but may behave with agency
- Often tied to historical or Blefelheim events
- Activation can be triggered by proximity, intent, or bloodline
- Some manifest only under certain conceptual conditions

### ▸ SUBTYPES

- Cognitively reactive objects
- Resonant relics from pre-collapse events
- Self-replicating constructs
- Bound-memory vessels

### ▸ RISK LEVEL

**Class II–V (Varies)**
Containment is usually structural or perceptual.

### ▸ NOTES

Artifacts are not tools. They are truths with mass.
If it speaks — don't answer. If it listens — don't think loudly.
