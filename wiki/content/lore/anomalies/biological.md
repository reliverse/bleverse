### ▸ TYPE

Biological Anomaly

### ▸ BEHAVIOR

Affects living organisms at the cellular, genetic, or cognitive-biological interface. Alters form, function, reproduction, or inter-species interaction.

### ▸ EFFECTS

- Morphogenic mutations (organisms change shape based on emotion or intent)
- Biofeedback contagion (pain or thoughts spread physically)
- Neural repurposing (brains rewire themselves unpredictably)
- Exo-conscious growth (tissues develop external sentience)
- Hybridization drift (spontaneous merging of unrelated species)
- Biological memory imprinting (flesh stores and transmits information)

### ▸ RISK LEVEL

**Class V – Adaptive Cascade Threat**
Can trigger uncontrolled organic propagation, cross-species anomalies, or sentient biomass emergence.
Containment requires biohazard quarantines and cognitive inoculation protocols.

### ▸ SUMMARY

A Biological Anomaly doesn't just infect — it transforms.
It treats life as raw code, rewriting bodies, thoughts, instincts.
In Blefelheim, evolution isn't natural — it's programmable.
And sometimes, it programs back.
