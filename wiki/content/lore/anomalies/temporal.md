### ▸ TYPE

Temporal Anomaly

### ▸ BEHAVIOR

Disrupts linear causality and temporal integrity. Affects perception of time, event order, and the relationship between cause and effect.

### ▸ EFFECTS

- Sequence corruption (events occur out of logical order)
- Temporal loops and recurrence fractures
- Memory desynchronization / collapse
- Causal bleed between timelines
- Delay or acceleration of perception

### ▸ RISK LEVEL

**Class IV – Recurrence Unstable**
High probability of localized loop generation or timeline instability. Immediate containment recommended.

### ▸ SUMMARY

A Temporal Anomaly defies conventional understanding of time.
It destabilizes continuity, alters historical certainty, and may render linear logic unreliable within affected zones.
