Bleverse offers a focused set of features designed around clarity and consent.

## Profile & Identity

Your profile is your home on Bleverse. You control what information you share and who can see it. Identity is persistent — you can build a coherent presence over time.

## Projects

Projects give you a place to showcase your work. Whether code, writing, art, or other creations, projects live alongside your profile. Visibility settings apply per project.

## Feed (Optional)

The feed is **optional**. Unlike platforms that push a feed by default, Bleverse lets you choose. If you enable it, you see content from people and projects you follow. The ranking is explainable — you can understand why something appears.

## Visibility Controls

Visibility is consistent and under your control:

- **Profile visibility** — Public, followers-only, or private
- **Project visibility** — Per-project settings
- **Unified privacy language** — Clear rules about who sees what

Settings are easy to find and reversible. Consent precedes automation — the system does not assume.
