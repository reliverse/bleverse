Bleverse is an open-source social platform at [bleverse.com](https://bleverse.com). It is built around clarity, consent, and modular architecture.

**Calm by default. Powerful by design.**

## A Different Kind of Social Network

Bleverse is designed for people who want:

- **Identity and projects in one place** — Your profile and work live together.
- **Control over attention** — Optional feed, explainable ranking, no dark patterns.
- **Transparency** — System actions are visible and reversible; personalization is explicit.
- **Accessibility** — Usable by default, for everyone.
