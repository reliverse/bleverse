**Why are Bleverse projects hosted under the Reliverse GitHub organization?**

Bleverse is developed and maintained by the **Reliverse** GitHub organization.

Reliverse is the technical and research layer behind Bleverse.

It provides the infrastructure, tools, and experimental technologies that power the Bleverse platform.

In simple terms:

- **Bleverse** is the platform and ecosystem.
- **Reliverse** is the GitHub organization building and maintaining it.

Some projects under Reliverse may explore independent ideas, research directions, or experimental technologies — but Bleverse remains one of its core initiatives.

## Philosophy

Reliverse focuses on building connective technologies — systems that enable communication, creativity, and interaction across digital spaces.

Bleverse is one of the primary environments where these technologies are applied in practice.
